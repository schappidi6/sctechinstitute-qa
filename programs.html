Diploma Programs

SC Technological Institute offers two diploma programs delivered 100% online via Moodle LMS with Zoom live instruction. Each program is 194 clock hours over 12 weeks.

Diploma

Artificial Intelligence Developer

194 Clock Hours (174 Theory + 20 Lab)
12 Weeks
100% Online
Total Cost: $6,900

Program Objective

This program is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience required to design, develop, and deploy AI-driven solutions across diverse industries.

Program Description

This 12-week program prepares individuals who desire to make an impact in the AI field to create real-world AI solutions to complex business or social issues.
