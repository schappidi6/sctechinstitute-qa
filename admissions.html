Admissions

Admission Requirements

To be admitted, applicants must:

  • Be 18 years of age (applicants under 18 require a parent or guardian signature)
  • Provide a complete and signed Enrollment Agreement
  • Provide a copy of a valid government-issued ID

Application Process

Follow these steps to begin your journey at SC Technological Institute.

1

Complete Application

Submit your application online or contact the admissions office directly.

2

Submit Documents

Provide your signed Enrollment Agreement and a copy of your government-issued ID.

3

Admission Decision

You will receive an email notification with your admission decision and further instructions.

4

Registration

An admissions agent will contact you to complete your registration process.

5

Orientation

Attend mandatory orientation for all new students before classes begin.

Payment Options

  • Full payment at the time of signing the enrollment agreement
  • Application fee at signing with the remaining balance paid prior to the starting date
  • Application fee at signing with the remaining balance paid prior to graduation via a payment plan

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Summary of Refund Policy

  • Cancel within 3 business days of signing: Full refund of all monies paid
  • Cancel after 3rd business day but before the first class session: Full refund minus the $150.00 non-refundable application fee
  • Cancel through 40% completion of the program: Pro rata refund based on the percentage of the program not completed
  • After 40% completion: No refund is issued
  • Refund timeline: All refunds are made within 30 days of cancellation or termination
