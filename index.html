Admissions

Is This Program Right for You?

Our programs are designed for working professionals and tech-adjacent candidates who are ready to deepen and formalize their skills in AI and data analytics.

Who Should Apply

  • Professionals with a background in technology, IT, or software
  • Career changers with hands-on experience in data, coding, or analysis
  • Graduates with degrees or coursework in STEM, computer science, or mathematics
  • IT professionals looking to formalize AI & analytics skills
  • Tech-adjacent workers ready for a structured, intensive upskill

Prerequisites

  • High school diploma or GED (required)
  • Basic computer literacy and internet proficiency
  • Prior exposure to technology, data, or programming concepts
  • Commitment to 20–25 hours per week of online study
  • Must be 18 years of age or older
