Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SCTI.

What programs do you offer?
SC Technological Institute offers two diploma programs: the Artificial Intelligence Developer diploma and the Data Analytics Developer diploma. Both programs are designed to provide industry-relevant skills for high-demand technology careers.
How much does the program cost?
The total program cost is $6,900. This covers tuition, course materials, and access to all online learning platforms. Contact our admissions team for details about payment options.
How long are the programs?
Each program is 12 weeks in duration, comprising 194 clock hours of instruction. Programs are delivered in 16-week online terms via Moodle LMS with synchronous instruction through Zoom.
Are classes online?
Yes, all classes are 100% online. Instruction is delivered via Moodle LMS with live synchronous sessions conducted through Zoom. Classes are held Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM EST. All sessions are recorded and available in the LMS for later review.
What are the admission requirements?
To be admitted to SCTI, applicants must:
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Complete and sign an Enrollment Agreement
  • Provide a valid Government-issued ID
